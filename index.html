An Introduction

At the start of the new millennium.

I was born on February 17th in the year 2000. I am the youngest is my family which consits of five members, my parents and my two older brothers. As of now I am currently a Computer Scientist student in my college named the Waterford Institute of Technology. Growing up in the 2000s was a special time for me as I got to see the rise of technology and the internet. According to my parents, I was quite a shy person when meeting new people especially in both pre-school and primary school. However I was very good at using technology.

My Mam recalls a time where my Dad had trouble finding a tv channel on the tv remote. So I help him by tinkering away with the remote myself as I was always watching tv. My brothers taught me a lot of good lessons growing up and protected me if I was in danger. I own them a lot because it was them who introduced me a lot of my favorites hobbies such a gaming and sports. As a 21 year old now, I do miss the simpler times but I have learn to accept things the way they are. Having childhood memories is great and all, but you should explore what is out there and make more memories.
